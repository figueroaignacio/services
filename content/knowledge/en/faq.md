# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## How long does it take to complete a project?
- **Starter Plan**: Usually takes 1 week from start to finish.
- **Pro Plan**: Usually takes 1 to 2 weeks.
- **Custom Plan**: Usually takes 2 to 4 weeks depending on the complexity of integrations and backend features.

## Why is there a monthly fee?
Unlike traditional agencies that dump the code on your lap and disappear, I manage the active lifecycle of your website. The monthly fee covers:
1. High-performance global hosting (fast loading speeds)
2. Custom domain name registration/renewal
3. SSL security certificates (required for secure browsing and Google SEO)
4. Active security monitoring, threat protection, and daily backups
5. Minor text, image, and style modifications on demand so you don't have to hire a developer for every tiny change.

## Do I get an administration panel to edit content?
Yes! In the **Pro** and **Custom** plans, I integrate a custom, lightweight admin panel (like Payload CMS or Content Collections) so you can update blogs, portfolio items, pricing, or team members on your own without writing a line of code.

## Can you connect the site to my existing tools?
Absolutely. I regularly integrate websites with CRM pipelines (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive), email marketing tools (Mailchimp, ConvertKit), instant messaging platforms (WhatsApp, Slack), and payment gateways (Stripe, Mercado Pago).
